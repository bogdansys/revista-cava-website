Poezie

Nothing scarier than change

things are different and i'm changed and it's all because of you
and this is strange and this is terrifying and this is new
but it's not that bad thinking i'm not alone
and we both are here struggling to come through.
it's barely been two months and most would say it's just confusion and incertitude
and yes i find myself thinking about it too
but how could i call myself confused when
my eyes smile before i do,
when my cheeks blush a little every time i see you
and my arms feel helpless,
when my heart says yes before even my brain can realize
what's happening to me


articol scris de Mihaela Filipescu
