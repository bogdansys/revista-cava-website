Limbi Straine

SHE

Has it ever happened to you to meet someone about whom you knew information beforehand? What if the information was negative? Will you be biased? Will you have a different approach than the usual one of seeing a complete stranger? The world we live in thrives off of human relationships. Rumors spread and alongside them lies coil around in the minds of listeners. However, there is a certain beauty in every chaos.

This article describes a real person from the perspective of one of the humans on whom she had a great influence.

The people you surround yourself with have a great impact on your life, bigger than you can imagine. She is a cataclysm. A positive one. She enters your life and once you give her the smallest leverage, she will grip onto it and hardly let go, if you mean something to her.

She is powerful and dominant, in the sense that her personality stands out. She will come up to you in a large crowd, introduce herself and start a conversation, even if that is the very first time you met her. Don’t ask her why, don’t assume she is flirting…just accept her hunger of knowing new people and diversifying her point of view.

She will look as though she is just having a casual conversation with you, but in reality you are being thoroughly analyzed. She then uses that information to differentiate types of people and later on give advice in similar situations. She’ll dissect even her closest friends. One night, while sitting on a balcony, the cold breeze of the sea brushing our cheeks, she revealed information about my past I hadn’t even thought about. All these years, as she was quietly listening, she was building up a case. She is mysterious and intelligent in ways you wouldn’t think about.

She opens up only to the people closest to her. When she loves you as a friend, she will dedicate all her time to you when you are together. She is of a rare loyalty, the kind that you only seem to find in movies or raunchy books.

She is a point of reference, an open history book, a pole of confidence. Her favorite painter is Hieronymus Bosch. She is deeply in love with “The Garden of Earthly Delights”, Hieronymus Bosch’s artistic apex. She went as far as to study the Dutchman finest brushstrokes, revealing to me details I wouldn’t have even perceived had it not been for her calculated, careful eye.

She is an artist, a painter, a fashion connoisseur. Her jewelry defines her. She loves wearing heavy silver rings. They vary in shapes, sizes and all from different parts of the world. She loves earrings and bracelets too, but not the simple kind. All her pieces turn heads on the street and it’s always figures in awe who admire her collection and are sometimes disappointed when they hear they have to go all the way to Ethiopia to even get the chance at finding a similar piece. She loves it. She loves all that’s opposite from the mundane. She wears embroidered vests and coats with white shirts underneath and long oriental necklaces. She then puts on her niche perfume, a name I never seem to remember, yet I always recognize the signature scent. Notes of nutmeg, cannabis, mandarin, orange and bergamot flavors fill the air she leaves behind.

Most of all, she is an avid reader. In fact, the idea of this article came to me while I was at a coffee shop with her and she recommended that I read “Walk through walls” by Marina Abramović, a captivating book I talked about in my last article. What fascinated me was the way she described it and how she made me feel hungry for more of it and adamant to own it myself. In fact, we were both so invested in the conversation, that a lady nearby started peeking at the cover, curious to know the title.

The perspective you have just read was from a platonic point of view. However, I couldn’t help but add the notes of someone else who was equally as impacted by her presence and developed feelings towards her. Here follow the words of that said person: “And even as a person whose presence you can indulge in romantically, she is easily one of the most complex people you could ever meet. It’s as if every day you get to meet a new version of her, calcified proof of the unstoppable tides of time, growing and changing by the hour. Eventually, you become part of this hypnotic dance, during which you face the choice of stagnating or evolving as your time with her goes on, choice that you would have to be a fool to pass up.

Her loving gaze seems to size you up and down and immediately find the root of whatever in the world it is that irks you. Her tenderness means she’s always there for you, no matter the time of day, but make no mistake, she makes sure to let her fiery side out every once in a while.

At the end of the day, there isn’t one aspect about her that I don’t love. Her presence, her way of being and how sensitive she is are all things that have changed my life in ways I hadn’t even thought were possible. And for that, I will forever be indebted to her. In many ways she is the reason I am still alive, here to write these words that do her no justice. I am deeply in love with her and truly believe that one day everyone deserves to meet someone as impactful as her.”

This article described the existence of someone very special. She is unique to the people who know her. For all we know, I may have just described someone in your life too or someone you are looking for. Truthfully, I consider myself lucky to have met her and so have the people I’ve spoken to. I wish there were words to provide a fitting end to this story, but as with every tale, the hero goes on to live many more years and to change the people she meets.
