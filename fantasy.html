On fantasy, escapism and fantasial worlds

There are few feelings that compare to being sucked into a book’s universe. And, in spite of all the differences between these wondrous worlds and the reality we have to face every day, fantasy novels just seem to get us hooked a bit quicker than any other genre, regardless of our age. But what is it exactly that makes us yearn for these stories filled with magical adventures and elements that, logically, we know are never going to happen? After all, it is much more likely that a romance novel, or even a murder mystery, should become relatable to our day to day existence and routine. So why do we set ourselves up for disappointment?

In order to have a better view on this subject, we need to take a closer look at some of the most successful fantasy novels of all time. Undoubtedly, one of the first that springs to mind is “Harry Potter”, perhaps alongside “The Chronicles of Narnia”, “Percy Jackson and the Olympians” and its spin-off series, as well as “The Lord of the Rings”. These are great fantasy series that have done remarkably well on the market and have passed the test of time, being appreciated by people of different generations, in spite of the genre being seen by many as childish. And although they take different approaches to the common theme of the alternate, better, magical universe where the heroes feel at home more than in the regular, boring, ordinary world, they have something else in common. Something that lies at the core of every good fantasy story – it provides an all-consuming reverie, which blurs the line between reality and fiction.

Whether it is a secret world of magic with a school for young witches and wizards, a parallel universe with kings and queens and talking animals or a camp where children of gods go for protection, all of these stories provide their main characters, who are regular kids, with a place to escape the depressing, cruel realities they have to face at home. The theme of escapism is perhaps the only thing, aside from magical aspects, that every story in this genre has in common. It is the first brick that all fantasy writers put in the foundation of the world they create.

It might also just be the reason why we enjoy fantasy so much. Freud believed that a dose of daydreaming and a getaway from the dullness and struggles of living is necessary for the human mind, and it can be provided in many ways. Movies, video games, books, they all give us a reprise from the hustle and bustle of our lives. They temporarily alleviate the worries of school, jobs and the pressures of academic achievement, and they instead provide magic, happiness and adventure. But this raises an even bigger question: why do we need to escape? Why can’t we just face our problems without needing these breaks?

According to some psychologists, it all stems from the innate fight or flight responses. The struggles of daily life are, after all, a threat to one’s mental health, being the catalyst for issues such as anxiety, severe stress and, in extreme cases, even depression, and that is when the basic behavioural patterns kick in. Some choose to deal with their problems as soon as these arise (fight), while others would much rather pretend like everything is still fine and try to find a way around confrontation (flight). Consequently, they develop different mechanisms to cope, all of which require a distraction from any social interaction that might remind them of the matter at hand. This is where fantasy swoops in and saves the day, whisking the reader away to far off places, battling dark witches, wizards and mythical creatures, blending the heroes in with the mundane and making it all tangible to the audience. And, in my opinion, this is the real magic.

In spite of knowing that these wonderful things are never going to happen in real life, we still can’t help but pick up that book with colourful, fantastical drawings on the cover, hoping that maybe a miracle will occur and magic will come our way some day. We still invest time and love in the stories and the characters, we still imagine ourselves in the hero’s place, and we do not care about the disappointments because we ought to have that bit of magic in our lives. Our need to break free from the ordinary far outweighs any disadvantages of falling in love with a world that we will never be able to set foot into. With days becoming more and more alike, with routine taking root deep within our modern lives and with technology doing most of our work, our minds are becoming increasingly bored and unsolicited. This is why fantasy novels are never going to be unappealing—they give us the adventure and excitement we no longer have in real life.

The rise of AOC - "A much needed change in Congress"

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, better known by her initials AOC, is an American member of the U.S House of Representatives for New York’s 14th congressional district since 2019 and part of the Democratic Party. At the age of only 31, she is known to be the youngest woman ever to serve in the United States Congress, having taken office at age 29, which has propelled her into the eyes of the younger generations. Another factor that has put the young Democrat in the spotlight is her unquestionable implication in the current global issues and her remarkable speeches, where she does not hold back on speaking her mind.

From having an asteroid named after her in high school for important research in the biological field to graduating from Boston University cum laude, Ocasio-Cortez has been working her way up ever since. Hired as a bartender and waitress, while also launching Brook Avenue Press and organizing Bernie Sanders’s (American politician, U.S. Senator from Vermont) presidential campaign, she started showing interest not only in politics but also in helping and supporting her community. After her Campaign in April 2017, when she was still waiting tables, she challenged Joe Crowley, the Democratic Caucus Chair, being the first person to do so since 2004. On June 26 2018 Ocasio-Cortez wins by almost 15 percentage points. The defeat of Rep. Crowley was the one that brought her nationwide attention.

Ever since then, she has attacked many difficult topics and made drastic changes for her community. She approached the issue of immigration with immediate action for the refugees at the border. The American Dream and Promise Act of 2019, which would financially help the immigrants, showed her implication in the issue. The U.S Democrat has also put a lot of accent on healthcare and the service provided to those with less financial possibilities. That is why she is an unapologetic advocate of Medicare for All, wanting to create a system in which every single person in America would have access to high-quality and consistent healthcare. She is also currently associated with Congressional Oversight and Government Reform Committee, facilitating the appearance of a generic version of PrEP, an HIV prevention drug by a year.

What sets young Rep.Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez apart from her fellow House members is the connection with her community and the interest she takes in matching as many generations as possible. In a committee of oversight and reform hearing, she spoke about the fact that the government has tried to undermine LGBTQ+ rights. She accuses lawmakers of “weaponizing and abusing scripture to justify bigotry” by using their Christian faith to marginalize communities such as LGBTQ+ people. She continues by stating “There is nothing holy about turning someone away from a hospital”, pointing out the white supremacy, racism, and homophobia, which is still present and tolerated in today’s society. “I am tired of it” AOC states, following her exposing of the corruption.

So far, Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez has not only been present in the public eye, during hearing, but also online. She has a considerable media influence, using Twitter and Instagram as her main platforms of communication. What has gained her a rapid following is the content she puts out on the media. Her Instagram stories are not only insiders into the Capitol in D.C, but also what the life of a U.S Representative looks like. Her videos do not portray a glamourized view of politics, but rather a raw image of the life in Congress as a young woman. She is set out to educate her community regarding their rights and laws, with constant detailed Q&A’s.

AOC has also acquired quite a lot of recognition from her remarkable tweets. One of her most significant fights which took place not only on Twitter, but also in a House Floor Speech is the famous ‘AOC vs. Rep.Yoho’. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez was verbally assaulted by fellow member Ted Yoho, as she was walking the stairs of the Capitol. He called her “crazy”, “disgusting”, “dangerous “for speaking on impunity. Later that day he proceeded to call her in front of the reporters “a f------b----“. The Congresswoman took this chance to address a serious issue of today’s society: abuse towards women. She pointed out the disrespect he has brought on the opposite sex. AOC continued to talk about the dehumanizing language in “a pattern of an attitude towards women and the dehumanization of others”. Following the manner of Yoho’s apology, Ocasio-Cortez says “to see the excuses and to see our Congress accept it as legitimate (…) I could not allow that to stand”, which is why she proceeds to call him out in the speech. “Treating people with dignity and respect makes a decent man” are the words she uses to expose the superior behavior of some men in Congress. As a conclusion to the incident, AOC does not only expose the misogyny and immaturity of her fellow members but also calls out real issues within the Congress.

As her achievements have shown, AOC is a determined feminist and evaluate of equality, fighting for her community and the well-being of the nation. She has inspired younger women to stand up for themselves and believe in their own strength. A strong advocate of the phrase “Femininity has power”, and constantly fighting for recognition in a domain, generally ruled by men, is what makes her a role model. The way in which she stands for her beliefs and takes action to prove her strength is what caught my attention, making me want to know more about her work. The term “self-made” is more than appropriate when it comes to describing AOC. Starting from the ground up, by waiting tables and trying to help her mom financially, after the passing of her father, she build her career on her own work. Earning her famous #aoc2024 on Twitter is one of the main aspects which show the incredible response to her actions. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez has never been afraid to pick a fight with anyone, no matter their status or power. Considering her achievements, many view her as a true model, including myself.
