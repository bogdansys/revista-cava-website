WINTER 2020 TRENDS

Even though the pandemic has been stopping us from enjoying our day to day lives, it has certainly not been an impediment to the appearance of new seasonal trends. From the omnipresent colour brown to matrix-style blazers, here I am presenting you the most wearable winter trends right off the runway or styled by other fashionistas. You can easily incorporate them in your wardrobe, while battling the cold, and still be in style.

1. BRINGING BACK THE 90’S and 2000’S

I know, I know, most people are allergic to the “Paris Hilton Era”, but hear me out: a respectable designer is always capable of convincing fashion enthusiasts that the newest trend is suitable for anyone.
